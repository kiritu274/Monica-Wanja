Called to Minister Through Music

Monica Wanja Kangara is a passionate gospel musician dedicated to spreading God's love through the power of music. With a heart for worship and a voice anointed by the Holy Spirit, she has been ministering to congregations and communities across Kenya and beyond.

Her music blends contemporary gospel with traditional African rhythms, creating a unique sound that touches hearts and draws people closer to Christ.
