Listen to My Music

Called to Minister Through Music

Monica Wanja Kangara is a passionate gospel musician dedicated to spreading God's love through the power of music. With a heart for worship and a voice anointed by the Holy Spirit, she has been ministering to congregations and communities across Kenya and beyond.

Her music blends contemporary gospel with traditional African rhythms, creating a unique sound that touches hearts and draws people closer to Christ. Through her ministry, Monica seeks to encourage believers, reach the lost, and glorify God in all she does.

Born and raised with a deep love for music and faith, Monica began her musical journey in church choirs before stepping into solo ministry. Her authentic worship style and heartfelt lyrics have blessed many lives and continue to impact the gospel music scene.
